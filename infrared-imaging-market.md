<p><strong>The Infrared Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cooled Infrared Imaging</li><li>Uncooled Infrared Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared imaging market is primarily divided into two types: cooled and uncooled infrared imaging. Cooled infrared imaging involves cooling the sensor to minimize thermal noise, enhancing sensitivity and image clarity, making it suitable for applications requiring high precision, like military and scientific use. Uncooled infrared imaging, on the other hand, operates at ambient temperatures, offering a cost-effective alternative for commercial and industrial applications, such as surveillance and automotive systems, where moderate sensitivity is sufficient.</p></p>
