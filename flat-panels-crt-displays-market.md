<p><p>The flat panel display market comprises three main types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) uses liquid crystals manipulated by electric fields, providing good color reproduction and energy efficiency. LED (Light Emitting Diode) is essentially an advanced form of LCD that utilizes LEDs for backlighting, enhancing brightness and contrast. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays feature self-emissive technology, allowing for deeper blacks and wider viewing angles due to each pixel emitting its own light, resulting in vibrant colors and energy savings.</p></p>
