<p><strong>The 3D Volumetric Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Volume Display</li><li>Solid-Volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D volumetric displays market comprises two key types: swept-volume displays and solid-volume displays. Swept-volume displays create 3D visuals by utilizing moving components, projecting images into a designated space, making them suitable for dynamic and interactive applications. In contrast, solid-volume displays provide a static 3D representation by rendering images throughout a solid medium, offering a tangible form and depth perception. Both types cater to diverse industries like healthcare, entertainment, and education, enhancing visualization and user engagement.</p></p>
