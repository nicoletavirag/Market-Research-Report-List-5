<p><p>The Lyocell fiber market is primarily divided into two types: Regular Lyocell Fiber and Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber. Regular Lyocell Fiber is known for its natural softness and breathability, making it suitable for clothing and home textiles. In contrast, Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber undergoes a chemical modification process, enhancing its durability and water resistance, which makes it ideal for applications requiring higher strength and moisture management. Both types underscore the versatility of Lyocell in sustainable textile solutions.</p></p>
