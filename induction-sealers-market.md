<p><p>Induction sealers are specialized devices used in the packaging industry to create tamper-evident seals on containers, primarily made of plastic or glass. These sealers utilize electromagnetic induction to heat a conductive foil liner applied to the container's opening, forming a hermetic seal when cooled. This process enhances product freshness, prevents leakage, and protects against contamination, making it vital in sectors such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p><p>The Induction Sealers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing consumer demand for enhanced product safety and quality assurance, particularly in food packaging. Recent trends indicate a shift toward automation and smart technology integration in sealing processes, improving efficiency and reducing labor costs.
