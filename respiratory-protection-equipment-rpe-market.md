<p><strong>The Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure APR</li><li>Positive Pressure PAPR</li><li>Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) is crucial for safeguarding individuals from airborne hazards. The market features several types: Negative Pressure Air-Purifying Respirators (APR) filter contaminants from the air, relying on the wearer’s inhalation to draw air through the filters. Positive Pressure Powered Air-Purifying Respirators (PAPR) use a battery-powered blower to provide a continuous flow of filtered air, ensuring a higher level of protection. Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) supplies breathable air from a tank, essential for environments with insufficient oxygen or high pollutants.</p></p>
