<p><p>The Non-Barrier Sealant Films market is categorized into mono-layer and multi-layer films. Mono-layer films consist of a single polymer layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but with limited performance in moisture and gas protection. In contrast, multi-layer films are composed of multiple layers of different materials, providing enhanced barrier properties, durability, and versatility for various packaging applications. These films are critical in sectors like food packaging and medical supplies, where product shelf life and safety are paramount.</p></p>
