<p><ul><li>YAG Field Lens(1064nm)</li><li>CO2 Field Lens(10.6μm)</li><li>Green Field Lens(532nm)</li><li>UV Field Lens(355nm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The field lens market encompasses various types of lenses optimized for specific wavelengths in laser applications. YAG field lenses operate at 1064nm, commonly used in industrial and medical lasers for high-power applications. CO2 field lenses at 10.6μm are ideal for cutting and engraving materials, particularly in manufacturing. Green field lenses operating at 532nm are prominent in laser displays and lighting. Finally, UV field lenses at 355nm are utilized in precision applications such as semiconductor processing and scientific research. Each lens type is tailored to enhance performance across its intended use cases.</p></p>
