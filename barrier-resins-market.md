<p><p>Barrier resins are specialized materials used to prevent gas and moisture transmission in packaging applications. EVOH (ethylene vinyl alcohol) resin offers excellent gas barrier properties, particularly against oxygen, making it ideal for food packaging. PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) provides strong moisture and oxygen barrier capabilities, often used in food and pharmaceutical packaging. PEN (polyethylene naphthalate) combines heat resistance with barrier properties, suitable for high-performance applications. Other types may include various blends or emerging polymers that enhance barrier performance for specific uses.</p></p>
