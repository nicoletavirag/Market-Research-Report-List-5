<p><strong>The Solid-State LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phase Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid-State LiDAR market comprises three primary types: MEMS-based scanning, phase array, and non-scanning flash. MEMS-based scanning utilizes micro-electromechanical systems to achieve precise, adjustable beam steering for detailed imaging. Phase array technology employs multiple small antennas to manipulate the phase of emitted signals, enabling rapid data acquisition without mechanical movement. Non-scanning flash LiDAR emits a broad light pulse to capture entire scenes simultaneously, allowing for fast, high-resolution mapping without mechanical components, enhancing durability and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
