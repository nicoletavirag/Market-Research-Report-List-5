<p><p>Battery cooling systems in electric vehicles (EVs) are essential for maintaining optimal battery performance and longevity. The air cooling system uses ambient air to dissipate heat, offering a lightweight and cost-effective solution, but may be less efficient in extreme conditions. In contrast, liquid cooling systems circulate a coolant around the battery, providing superior thermal management and ensuring consistent temperatures, making them more effective for high-performance or larger EV applications. Each system has its advantages and market suitability based on vehicle requirements.</p></p>
