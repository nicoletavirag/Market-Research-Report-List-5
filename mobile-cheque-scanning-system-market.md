Single-Feed systems allow users to scan one cheque at a time, making them suitable for low-volume transactions and personal use. In contrast, Multi-Feed systems can process multiple cheques simultaneously, catering to businesses and financial institutions that handle high volumes of deposits efficiently. Both systems enhance convenience and speed in cheque processing, aligning with the growing demand for digital banking solutions.</p></p>
