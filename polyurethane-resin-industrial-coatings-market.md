<p><p>Polyurethane resin industrial coatings market can be divided into two main types - solvent based coating and waterborne coating. Solvent based coatings are traditional and widely used, offering excellent durability and resistance to harsh environments. However, they can be harmful to the environment and human health due to volatile organic compounds. On the other hand, waterborne coatings are environmentally friendly, emitting lower levels of VOCs and providing good performance in industrial applications. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
