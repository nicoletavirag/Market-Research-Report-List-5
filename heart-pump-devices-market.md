<p><strong>The Heart Pump Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Heart Pump Devices</li><li>Extracorporeal Heart Pump Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart pump devices market comprises two main types: implantable heart pump devices and extracorporeal heart pump devices. Implantable devices are surgically placed within the body to support or replace the heart's function, often used in patients with severe heart failure. In contrast, extracorporeal devices are external machines that temporarily assist or take over the heart's pumping action, typically employed during surgeries or emergency situations. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes and quality of life by enhancing cardiac function.</p></p>
