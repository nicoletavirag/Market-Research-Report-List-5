<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Flooring (LVT) is a popular flooring option known for its durability and aesthetic appeal. It comes in several types: </p><p>1. **Dry-Back LVT** is glued directly to the subfloor, offering stability.</p><p>2. **Loose-Lay LVT** utilizes a non-adhesive installation method, allowing easy removal and replacement.</p><p>3. **SPC (Stone Plastic Composite)** features a rigid core for enhanced durability and moisture resistance.</p><p>4. **WPC (Wood Plastic Composite)** provides a softer underfoot feel and is ideal for areas prone to moisture. Each type caters to different installation and performance needs.</p></p>
