<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radiotherapy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiotherapy equipment market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment** - These devices deliver radiation from outside the body, targeting tumors while minimizing damage to surrounding tissues.</p><p>2. **Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment** - Also known as brachytherapy, this method involves placing radioactive sources directly within or near the tumor for localized treatment.</p><p>3. **Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment** - This approach uses radioactive substances injected into the bloodstream, allowing for targeted treatment of cancer that has spread throughout the body. Each type offers unique advantages for diverse treatment scenarios.</p></p>
