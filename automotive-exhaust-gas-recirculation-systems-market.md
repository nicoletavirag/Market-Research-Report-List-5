<p><p>The automotive exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) systems market includes two primary types: pneumatic and electric EGR valves. Pneumatic EGR valves utilize pressure differentials to control exhaust gas flow, making them simpler and cost-effective, ideal for conventional internal combustion engines. In contrast, electric EGR valves offer more precise control through electric actuators, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions, which is increasingly important in modern vehicles. Both types are essential for meeting stringent environmental regulations while improving engine performance.</p></p>
