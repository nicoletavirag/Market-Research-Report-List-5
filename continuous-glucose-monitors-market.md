<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGMs) are medical devices that provide real-time tracking of glucose levels in individuals, particularly those with diabetes. They consist of a small sensor placed under the skin, which measures interstitial glucose levels and transmits the data to a receiver or smartphone. CGMs enhance diabetes management by allowing for timely adjustments to diet, medication, and lifestyle, thereby improving patient outcomes.</p><p>The Continuous Glucose Monitors Market is poised for significant growth, expected to expand at a CAGR of 9.90% during the forecast period. <p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGMs) are classified into wearable and implantable types. Wearable CGMs are externally attached to the body, using sensors to track glucose levels through the skin, allowing for real-time data display on smartphones or receivers. Implantable CGMs, on the other hand, are surgically inserted under the skin for long-term monitoring, providing more stable readings and reducing the need for frequent sensor changes. Both types enhance diabetes management by offering continuous monitoring and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
