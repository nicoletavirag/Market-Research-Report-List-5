<p><p>The small brushless motors market is primarily segmented into outer rotor and inner rotor types. Outer rotor motors feature the rotor on the outside, providing a larger surface area for cooling and often higher torque ratings, making them suitable for applications like drones and fans. In contrast, inner rotor motors have the rotor inside the stator, allowing for compact design and higher speeds, making them ideal for portable devices and precision tools. Both types serve distinct applications based on performance and size requirements.</p></p>
