<p><p>The NEV traction motor core market primarily encompasses two types: permanent magnet motor cores and AC induction motor cores. Permanent magnet motor cores utilize high-energy magnets to enhance efficiency and power density, making them ideal for electric vehicles that require compact designs. In contrast, AC induction motor cores rely on electromagnetic induction to generate motion, offering robustness and lower costs. Each type serves distinct applications within the new energy vehicle sector, influencing performance, range, and manufacturing considerations.</p></p>
