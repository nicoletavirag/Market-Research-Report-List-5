<p><p>The Automotive Brake Lining Market includes Semimetal and NAO (Non-Asbestos Organic) types. Semimetal linings, made from a blend of metals, fibers, and friction modifiers, offer high durability, heat resistance, and performance under heavy loads, making them ideal for commercial vehicles. NAO linings, composed of organic materials like glass, rubber, and resins, provide quieter operation, reduced wear on brake rotors, and eco-friendliness, suitable for passenger cars. Both types cater to diverse vehicle needs, balancing performance, safety, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
