<p><ul><li>Film-Based Imaging</li><li>Digital Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray inspection system market is divided into two main types: film-based imaging and digital imaging. Film-based imaging utilizes traditional X-ray films to capture images, relying on chemical processing for development. This method is often slower and less efficient, but can provide high-quality images. Digital imaging, on the other hand, employs advanced detectors and software to create images instantly, allowing for quicker analysis and easier storage. Digital systems enhance image quality, reduce radiation exposure, and integrate with modern imaging technologies.</p></p>
