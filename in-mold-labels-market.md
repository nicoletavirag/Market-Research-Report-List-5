<p><p>The In-Mold Labels (IML) market consists of three main types based on the molding process. Injection Molding IML incorporates labels during the injection process, creating a strong bond between the label and the molded part. Blow Molding IML involves inserting labels in the blowing process, ideal for hollow containers. Thermoforming IML applies labels during the heating and forming phase, suitable for packaging and trays. Each type offers distinct advantages in durability, aesthetics, and production efficiency, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
