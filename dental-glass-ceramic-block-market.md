<p><p>The dental glass ceramic block market is primarily categorized into transparent and opaque types. Transparent glass ceramics are favored for their aesthetic qualities, closely resembling natural tooth enamel, making them ideal for veneers and restorations requiring high translucency. In contrast, opaque glass ceramics are designed for applications where greater masking ability is needed, such as in crowns where underlying tooth color needs to be concealed. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to different patient needs and clinical requirements in dentistry.</p></p>
