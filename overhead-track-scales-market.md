<p><p>Overhead track scales are designed to measure the weight of heavy loads such as vehicles or materials in transit. Static scales provide precise weight measurements when items are stationary, ideal for loading docks and warehouses, ensuring accurate weight recording during transfer. Both types cater to different operational needs within logistics and manufacturing industries, enhancing efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
