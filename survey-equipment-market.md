Theodolites measure horizontal and vertical angles, while levels determine height differences. 3D laser scanners capture detailed spatial data quickly. Lasers are used for range finding and alignment tasks. Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) provide aerial imagery and data collection, enhancing surveying efficiency and coverage.</p></p>
