<p><ul><li>Mechanical Switches</li><li>Non-Contact Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The miniature door switches market comprises two main types: mechanical switches and non-contact switches. Mechanical switches operate through physical interaction, where a lever or button activates the switch when pressed, making them reliable in various applications. Non-contact switches, in contrast, utilize proximity sensors or magnetic fields to detect the presence or movement of an object without physical contact. Both types offer distinct advantages, such as ease of installation, durability, and suitability for different operational requirements in security and automation systems.</p></p>
