<p><p>Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) in the Electrical and Electronics market encompasses various specialized tools and methodologies that enhance design, development, and production efficiency. cPDM (collaborative Product Data Management) facilitates teamwork on product designs. CAD (Computer-Aided Design) aids in creating precise engineering drawings. EDA (Electronic Design Automation) focuses on circuit design and simulation. FEA (Finite Element Analysis) assesses material performance under stress. NC (Numerical Control) manages machine tool automation, while DM (Document Management) organizes project documentation and CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) analyzes fluid flow and thermal management.</p></p>
