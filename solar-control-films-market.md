<p><p>The solar control films market encompasses three main types: Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective) films. Clear films provide minimal visibility disruption while blocking heat and UV rays. Dyed films use color additives to reduce glare and heat without reflectivity, making them popular for aesthetics. Vacuum coated films, on the other hand, possess a reflective coating that enhances solar heat rejection and privacy, making them effective for energy efficiency and glare reduction in various settings.</p></p>
