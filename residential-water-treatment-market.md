<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point of Entry (POE)</li><li>Point of Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential water treatment solutions can be categorized into two main types: Point of Entry (POE) and Point of Use (POU). POE systems treat water at the main entry point of a home, providing clean water to all faucets and appliances. On the other hand, POU systems are installed at specific points in the home, such as under the sink or on the countertop, to provide clean water for drinking and cooking. Both types of systems play a crucial role in ensuring safe and clean water for residential use.</p></p>
