<p><p>The real-time PCR (RT-PCR) fluorescence probe market includes various types such as TaqMan, Molecular Beacons, and Minor Groove Binder Oligodeoxynucleotide Conjugates (MGB-ODN). TaqMan probes utilize a degradation mechanism to emit fluorescence during DNA amplification. Molecular Beacons are hairpin-shaped probes that fluoresce upon hybridization with target sequences. MGB-ODNs enhance specificity and sensitivity by binding to the minor groove of DNA. These technologies enable precise quantification of genetic material, facilitating advancements in research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
