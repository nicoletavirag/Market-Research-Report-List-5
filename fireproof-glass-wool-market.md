Glass wool boards are rigid insulation panels ideal for thermal and acoustic applications. Glass wool tubes are pre-formed insulating materials, commonly used to wrap pipes and HVAC systems for energy efficiency and fire resistance. Glass wool felt is a flexible, blanket-like material suited for various applications, including industrial insulation and soundproofing, offering excellent thermal protection and lightweight properties.</p></p>
