<p><p>SEBS, or Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene, is categorized into two main market types: linear SEBS and radial SEBS. Linear SEBS features a straight-chain structure, providing excellent elasticity and flexibility, making it suitable for applications like adhesives and sealants. In contrast, radial SEBS has a star-like molecular structure, offering enhanced thermal stability and strength, ideal for applications in automotive parts and consumer goods. Both types cater to diverse industries, leveraging their unique properties to meet specific market demands efficiently.</p></p>
