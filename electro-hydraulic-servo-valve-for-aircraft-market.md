<p><p>Electro Hydraulic Servo Valves (EHSV) in the aircraft market include three main types: Nozzle Flapper Valves, Jet Action Valves, and Direct Drive Valves. Nozzle Flapper Valves use a flapper mechanism to control fluid flow, allowing precise movement. Jet Action Valves utilize jets of hydraulic fluid for fast response and high control accuracy. Direct Drive Valves eliminate the need for mechanical linkages, offering quicker response times and improved reliability. These technologies are essential for managing aircraft systems, enhancing control and performance.</p></p>
