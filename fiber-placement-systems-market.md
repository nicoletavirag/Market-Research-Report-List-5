Vertical AFP systems employ a vertical setup for laying fibers, allowing for efficient production of complex geometries, often preferred in aerospace components. Conversely, Horizontal AFP systems utilize a horizontal configuration, which is advantageous for larger, flat parts, enabling easier access and faster material flow. Both types provide precision in composite manufacturing, catering to diverse industrial applications with specific production needs.</p></p>
