<p><p>Child-resistant packaging is designed to prevent children from easily accessing potentially harmful products. It includes reclosable packaging, which allows for convenient resealing after initial use, ideal for items like household chemicals or medications that may require continued access. Non-reclosable packaging, on the other hand, is intended for single use and securely seals the contents, often used for products like certain pharmaceuticals. Special blister packaging offers a unique solution, providing a tamper-proof and child-resistant barrier for individual doses, ensuring safety and maintaining product integrity.</p></p>
