<p><p>The acetylacetone market is primarily classified into two types: keto and enol. The keto form is a stable structure characterized by a carbonyl group, making it useful in various industrial applications, including chemical synthesis and as a catalyst. Conversely, the enol form features a hydroxyl group and is known for its strong chelating abilities with metal ions, making it valuable in agrochemicals and pharmaceuticals. Both forms play crucial roles in various sectors, influencing market dynamics and demand.</p></p>
