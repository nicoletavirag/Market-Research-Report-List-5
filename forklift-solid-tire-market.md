<p><ul><li>Cured-On Solid Tire</li><li>Pressed-On Solid Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forklift solid tires are widely available in two main types: Cured-On Solid Tires and Pressed-On Solid Tires. Cured-On Solid Tires are manufactured by curing rubber on a metal band, providing a durable and long-lasting option. Pressed-On Solid Tires are made by pressing a rubber tire onto a metal wheel, offering a cost-effective and easy-to-install alternative. Both types of solid tires offer excellent stability and performance for forklifts, catering to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
