<p><p>The panel-mounted timing relay market is segmented into three main types: on-delay, off-delay, and others. On-delay timers activate after a preset duration, ensuring that a device only operates after the specified time has elapsed. Off-delay relays maintain contact until the timing period concludes, allowing devices to continue functioning for a set time after the trigger has ceased. Other types may include mixed-function timers or programmable options, catering to diverse industrial applications and automation needs.</p></p>
