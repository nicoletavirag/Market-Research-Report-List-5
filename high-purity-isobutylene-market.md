<p><strong>The High Purity Isobutylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MTBE Decomposition Method</li><li>C4 Fraction Separation Method</li><li>Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Purity Isobutylene market comprises three primary production methods. The MTBE Decomposition Method involves breaking down methyl tertiary-butyl ether to extract isobutylene. The C4 Fraction Separation Method isolates isobutylene from a C4 hydrocarbon stream, effectively separating various components. Lastly, the Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method converts isobutane into isobutylene through a catalytic process, enhancing yield and purity. Each method offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and product quality, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
