<p><strong>The Capacitive Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Type</li><li>Absolute Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive hygrometers market is primarily divided into two types: relative and absolute. Relative type hygrometers measure the humidity level in relation to the maximum moisture the air can hold at a given temperature, providing a percentage value. In contrast, absolute type hygrometers measure the actual amount of water vapor present in the air, usually expressed in grams per cubic meter. Both types are essential for various applications, including industrial processes, meteorology, and HVAC systems, catering to different measurement needs.</p></p>
