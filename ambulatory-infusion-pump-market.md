<p><ul><li>Mechanics Pump</li><li>Electronic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ambulatory infusion pump market comprises two main types: mechanical pumps and electronic pumps. Mechanical pumps use manual mechanisms, such as gear systems, to deliver medication at a set rate, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, electronic pumps incorporate advanced technology with programmable settings, allowing for precise control, dosage customization, and features like alarms and monitoring capabilities. This differentiation enables healthcare providers to select pumps based on patient needs, treatment complexity, and desired levels of automation in medication administration.</p></p>
