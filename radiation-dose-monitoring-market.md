<p><p>The radiation dose monitoring market primarily includes dosimeters and area process monitors. Dosimeters are personal devices worn by individuals to measure exposure to ionizing radiation over time, ensuring safety in environments such as hospitals and nuclear facilities. Area process monitors, on the other hand, are fixed installations that continuously measure radiation levels in specific locations, providing real-time data to detect potential hazards and maintain compliance with safety regulations. Together, they play vital roles in radiation protection and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
