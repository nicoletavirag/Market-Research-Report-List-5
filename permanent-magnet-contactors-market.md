<p><p>AC contactors are used for controlling alternating current loads, while DC contactors are used for controlling direct current loads. AC contactors are predominantly used in industries due to the widespread use of AC power sources. On the other hand, DC contactors are commonly used in applications where a DC power source is required, such as in telecommunications, automotive, and aerospace industries. Both types of contactors serve the same purpose of controlling electrical currents, but they are designed to work with different types of power sources.</p></p>
