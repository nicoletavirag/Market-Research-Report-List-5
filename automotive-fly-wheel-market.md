<p><strong>The Automotive Fly Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single mass flywheel</li><li>Dual mass flywheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flywheel market consists of two primary types: single mass flywheels and dual mass flywheels. Single mass flywheels are simpler and more robust, typically used in performance and racing applications due to their lighter weight and direct power transfer. In contrast, dual mass flywheels feature two separate masses allowing for greater damping of vibrations and improved comfort, making them ideal for diesel engines and vehicles focused on smooth driving. Both types cater to distinct market needs and engineering preferences.</p></p>
