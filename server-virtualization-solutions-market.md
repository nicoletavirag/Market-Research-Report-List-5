<p><p>The server virtualization solutions market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based virtualization allows organizations to host virtual servers on remote data centers, providing scalability, flexibility, and reduced infrastructure costs. This model is ideal for businesses seeking quick deployment and easy management. On-premises virtualization, on the other hand, involves deploying virtual servers within an organization's own data center, offering greater control and security, but often requiring higher upfront investment and ongoing maintenance efforts.</p></p>
