<p><p>The wire covering compound market is divided into two main types: halogenated polymers and non-halogenated polymers. Halogenated polymers contain halogen elements, which enhance flame resistance but may release toxic gases during combustion. Non-halogenated polymers, on the other hand, do not include halogens, making them safer and more environmentally friendly, with lower emissions when exposed to fire. Both types cater to different applications, balancing safety, performance, and environmental impact in wire and cable insulation.</p></p>
