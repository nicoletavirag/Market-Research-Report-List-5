<p><ul><li>Water-fast</li><li>Non-water-tight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fast Curing Adhesive Market is divided into two main types: water-fast and non-water-tight adhesives. Water-fast adhesives are designed to resist moisture, making them ideal for applications exposed to water and high humidity, such as in construction or marine environments. Non-water-tight adhesives, on the other hand, provide quick bonding but are not suitable for wet conditions, making them more appropriate for indoor use or temporary fixes. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs, enhancing efficiency and durability.</p></p>
