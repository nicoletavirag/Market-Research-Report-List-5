<p><ul><li>Open Loop Stents</li><li>Closed-Loop Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-expandable intracranial stents market is categorized into open-loop and closed-loop stents. Open-loop stents feature a design that allows for greater flexibility and ease of placement within the vascular system, enhancing blood flow and reducing the risk of occlusion. In contrast, closed-loop stents have a more defined structure, ensuring stability and strength in maintaining vessel patency. Both types are crucial for treating conditions such as intracranial aneurysms and stenosis, catering to specific clinical needs.</p></p>
