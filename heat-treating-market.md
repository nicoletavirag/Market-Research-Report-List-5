<p><p>The heat treating market is categorized into three main types: Integral Heat Treatment, Surface Heat Treatment, and Chemical Heat Treatment. Integral Heat Treatment involves altering the material's properties throughout its volume, enhancing overall strength and durability. Surface Heat Treatment focuses on modifying just the outer layer, improving wear resistance and corrosion protection. Chemical Heat Treatment utilizes chemical processes, like nitriding or carburizing, to enhance surface characteristics. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, ensuring optimized performance of materials.</p></p>
