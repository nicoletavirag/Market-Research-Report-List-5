<p><ul><li>LCD</li><li>OLED</li><li>E-Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transparent display market encompasses three primary types: LCD, OLED, and E-Paper. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) technology utilizes a backlight and liquid crystals to create images while allowing some light to pass through, achieving partial transparency. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays provide enhanced transparency and vibrant colors without a backlight, as each pixel emits light independently. E-Paper, designed for low power consumption, mimics traditional paper and offers excellent visibility in various lighting conditions, making it ideal for signage and digital labels.</p></p>
