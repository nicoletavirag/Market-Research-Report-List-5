<p><p>The biogas upgrader market consists of several technologies for enhancing biogas quality. Membrane separation utilizes selectively permeable membranes to separate methane from carbon dioxide and other impurities, offering a compact, efficient solution. Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) relies on pressure changes to adsorb contaminants, effectively purifying biogas in larger installations. Other methods may include water scrubbing and chemical absorption, each providing unique advantages depending on the scale and specific application of biogas purification. Together, these technologies cater to diverse energy needs.</p></p>
