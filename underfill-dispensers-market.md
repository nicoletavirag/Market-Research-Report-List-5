<p><strong>The Underfill Dispensers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Flow Underfill</li><li>No Flow Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfill dispensers are used in semiconductor packaging to enhance reliability. The three main types are:</p><p>1. **Capillary Flow Underfill**: Utilizes capillary action to fill gaps between the chip and substrate, ensuring strong adhesion and thermal stability.</p><p>2. **No Flow Underfill**: A more viscous material that remains in place without flowing; it provides robust protection during assembly and is ideal for high-density applications.</p><p>3. **Molded Underfill**: Involves molding techniques to create a solid layer of underfill, offering additional mechanical support and reduced thermal mismatch.</p></p>
