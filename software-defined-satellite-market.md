<p><ul><li>Medium Earth Orbit</li><li>Low Earth Orbit</li><li>Geosynchronous Earth Orbit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The software-defined satellite market encompasses three primary types based on orbital position: Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), and Geosynchronous Earth Orbit (GEO). LEO satellites offer low-latency services ideal for applications like broadband and IoT; MEO satellites, positioned between LEO and GEO, provide navigation and communication solutions with moderate latency; while GEO satellites maintain a fixed position relative to the Earth, delivering wide coverage for broadcasting and telecommunications. Each type serves distinct market needs and application scenarios.</p></p>
