<p><p>The bioenzymes market includes various types, each serving distinct functions. Pectinase breaks down pectin in fruit processing, enhancing juice extraction. Lipase aids in fat digestion and is crucial in food and pharmaceutical industries. Protease facilitates protein breakdown, vital for meat tenderizing and brewing. Cellulase decomposes cellulose, enhancing biofuel production and waste management. Catalase neutralizes harmful hydrogen peroxide, important in biotechnology. Amylase converts starches into sugars, essential in baking and fermentation. These enzymes are integral to numerous industrial applications.</p></p>
