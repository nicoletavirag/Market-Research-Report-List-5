<p><p>The powdered cheese market can be classified into two main types: Pure Cheese Powder and Cheese Powder Blends. Pure Cheese Powder consists of solely cheese that has been dehydrated and pulverized into a fine powder, while Cheese Powder Blends are a mixture of cheese powder with other seasonings or ingredients to create unique flavors. Both types of powdered cheese are commonly used in various food products such as snacks, seasonings, sauces, and dips for their convenient storage, longer shelf life, and easy incorporation into recipes.</p></p>
