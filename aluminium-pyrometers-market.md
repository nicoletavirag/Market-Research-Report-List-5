<p><p>Aluminium pyrometers are temperature measurement devices used in various industrial applications, with two primary market types: fixed and portable. Fixed pyrometers are installed in a specific location, offering continuous monitoring of temperatures in processes like metal casting and forging. In contrast, portable pyrometers are handheld devices that enable flexibility, allowing operators to measure temperatures in multiple locations, making them ideal for maintenance and inspection tasks. Both types are essential for ensuring quality control and operational efficiency in aluminium production.</p></p>
