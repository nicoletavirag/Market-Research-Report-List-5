<p><p>The colostrum powder market is primarily divided into two types: whole colostrum powder and skim colostrum powder. Whole colostrum powder retains all the components of colostrum, including fats, proteins, and antibodies, making it rich in nutrients. It is highly sought after for its health benefits, particularly in immune support. Skim colostrum powder, on the other hand, has had most of the fat removed, resulting in a lower-calorie option that still offers many of the same nutritional benefits, catering to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
