<p><p>Enhanced Fire Protection Systems market consists of two main types: Active Systems and Passive Systems. Active Systems include fire alarms, sprinkler systems, and fire suppression systems that actively detect and extinguish fires. Passive Systems include fire-resistant materials, fire doors, and fire-rated glass that contain and limit the spread of fire. Both types work together to provide comprehensive protection for buildings and occupants. The Active Systems detect and respond to fires immediately, while Passive Systems help to prevent the fire from spreading and causing extensive damage.</p></p>
