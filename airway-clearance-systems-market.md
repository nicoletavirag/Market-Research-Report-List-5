<p><p>Airway clearance systems are medical devices used to help clear mucus and secretions from the lungs and airways. Some common types include Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) devices, which use resistance to help keep airways open during exhalation; Oscillatory Positive Expiratory Pressure (OPEP) devices, which provide vibrations to help loosen mucus; High Frequency Chest Wall Compression devices, which use rapid chest compressions to help clear mucus; Intrapulmonary Percussive Ventilation devices, which deliver rapid bursts of air to the lungs; and Mechanical Cough Assist devices, which help simulate a cough to clear secretions.</p></p>
