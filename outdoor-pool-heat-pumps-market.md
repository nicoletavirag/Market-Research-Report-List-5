<p><p>The outdoor pool heat pump market is comprised of three main types: geothermal, air source, and water source heat pumps. Geothermal heat pumps utilize the stable underground temperature to heat and cool pools efficiently. Air source heat pumps extract heat from the ambient air, making them versatile and cost-effective for various climates. Water source heat pumps draw heat from nearby water bodies, offering high efficiency. Each type provides unique advantages, catering to different user needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
