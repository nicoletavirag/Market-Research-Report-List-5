<p><p>Chromatographic silica resins are categorized by particle size ranges, each suited for specific applications. The **30-60** range offers larger particles, ideal for preparative chromatography with high flow rates and lower resolution. **60-100** provides a balance between resolution and flow rate, commonly used in analytical and semi-preparative chromatography. **100-200** delivers finer particles for high-resolution analytical applications. **Above 200** features ultra-fine particles, enabling exceptional resolution for advanced analytical techniques like UHPLC. Each type caters to distinct separation needs, optimizing efficiency and performance in chromatography workflows.</p></p>
