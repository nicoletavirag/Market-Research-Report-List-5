<p><p>The infrared thermal imaging camera market is primarily divided into two types: cooled and uncooled. Cooled type cameras use cryogenic cooling systems to enhance sensitivity, making them ideal for high-performance applications like defense and aerospace. They provide superior image quality but are typically more expensive and require maintenance. Uncooled type cameras, on the other hand, operate at ambient temperatures and are more compact and cost-effective, making them suitable for commercial use, building inspections, and temperature monitoring in various industries.</p></p>
