<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PTFE Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous PTFE Glass Fabric</li><li>Non-Porous PTFE Glass Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE fabric market comprises two primary types: porous PTFE glass fabric and non-porous PTFE glass fabric. Porous PTFE glass fabric features microscopic openings that allow the passage of air and moisture, making it ideal for applications requiring breathability, such as protective clothing and filtration materials. In contrast, non-porous PTFE glass fabric is completely impermeable, offering superior chemical resistance and durability, suitable for applications in harsh environments like industrial equipment and gaskets. Both types are prized for their exceptional heat and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
