<p><p>Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) devices for Parkinson's disease can be classified into single-channel and dual-channel systems. Single-channel DBS involves a single lead that delivers electrical impulses to a specific brain region, effectively managing symptoms with simpler designs. Conversely, dual-channel DBS utilizes two leads, allowing for stimulation of multiple areas simultaneously, enhancing therapeutic outcomes and providing more tailored treatments. Both types aim to improve motor control, reduce tremors, and enhance the quality of life for patients.</p></p>
