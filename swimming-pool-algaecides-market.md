<p><p>The swimming pool algaecide market comprises several types, each targeting algae growth effectively. Quat pool algaecides use quaternary ammonium compounds for fast-acting results but may foam. Polymer or polyquat algaecides are less likely to irritate skin and are effective in various conditions. Metallic algaecides leverage copper or silver for long-lasting control but can stain. Sodium bromide, often used in saltwater pools, serves as a preventive measure against algae growth, ensuring clear and clean water.</p></p>
