<p><p>The PFPE (Perfluoropolyether) market is divided into two main types: PFPE oil and PFPE grease. PFPE oil is primarily used for lubrication in high-temperature and chemically aggressive environments, providing excellent stability and durability. PFPE grease, on the other hand, is a thicker formulation that combines PFPE oils with thickeners, offering enhanced lubrication and sealing properties for long-lasting performance. Both types are essential in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and automotive for their superior thermal and chemical resistance.</p></p>
