<p><p>Cake toppers come in various types catering to different consumer preferences. Edible toppers are made from ingredients like fondant, sugar, or chocolate which are meant to be consumed along with the cake. On the other hand, not edible toppers are typically made from materials like plastic, wood, or acrylic and are meant for decorative purposes only. Both types cater to different needs, with edible toppers adding both aesthetic appeal and flavor to the cake, while not edible toppers provide a long-lasting decorative element.</p></p>
