<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crystallizable Shape Memory Polymers (cSMPs) are categorized into two main types based on their recovery behaviors and thermal properties. Type I cSMPs exhibit a single-shape recovery mechanism, transitioning from a temporary shape to a permanent one upon heating. Conversely, Type II cSMPs have a dual-shape memory behavior, allowing them to remember two distinct shapes, enhancing their versatility. These types cater to different applications, from soft robotics to medical devices, driven by their unique thermal responsiveness and mechanical properties.</p></p>
