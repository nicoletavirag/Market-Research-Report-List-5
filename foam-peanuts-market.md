<p><p>The foam peanuts market comprises two main types: biodegradable foam peanuts and regular foam peanuts. Biodegradable foam peanuts are designed to break down naturally over time, making them an environmentally friendly packaging option. They are typically made from natural starches and dissolve in water. On the other hand, regular foam peanuts are made from polystyrene and provide excellent protection for fragile items but are not biodegradable. The choice between these types often depends on environmental considerations and packaging needs.</p></p>
