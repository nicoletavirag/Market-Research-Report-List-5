<p><p>The packaging robots market is divided into primary and secondary packaging types. Primary packaging involves directly encasing individual products, such as filling, sealing, and wrapping operations, ensuring products are protected and presented for sale. Secondary packaging, on the other hand, focuses on grouping multiple primary packages for shipping and retail display, including tasks like boxing, palletizing, or shrink-wrapping. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve product handling in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
