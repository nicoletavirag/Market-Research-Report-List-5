<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Refrigeration Machinery & Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Refrigeration Machine</li><li>Absorption Refrigeration Machine</li><li>Steam Jet Refrigeration Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigeration machinery and equipment market is primarily categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Compression Refrigeration Machines** utilize mechanical compressors to pressurize refrigerants, facilitating heat exchange and cooling.</p><p>2. **Absorption Refrigeration Machines** rely on an absorption process, using heat to drive the refrigeration cycle, often utilizing natural gas or waste heat.</p><p>3. **Steam Jet Refrigeration Machines** operate by using steam to create a low-pressure environment, allowing vaporized refrigerant to absorb heat. Each type serves specific applications across various industries, optimizing energy use and cooling efficiency.</p></p>
