<p><ul><li>Evacuated Tube</li><li>Flat Plate</li><li>Unglazed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-pressurized solar water heater market includes three main types: evacuated tube, flat plate, and unglazed collectors. Evacuated tube systems utilize multiple glass tubes, enhancing efficiency by reducing heat loss, suitable for colder climates. Flat plate collectors consist of a dark-colored, insulated flat surface, ideal for moderate climates, as they are cost-effective and straightforward. Unglazed collectors, usually made of dense plastic, are often used for applications like pool heating in warm areas, providing basic heating without significant investment.</p></p>
