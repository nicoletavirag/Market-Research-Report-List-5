<p><strong>The Inflight WIFI Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATG</li><li>Ka Band Satellite</li><li>Ku Band Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inflight WiFi equipment can be categorized into three main types: ATG, Ka Band Satellite, and Ku Band Satellite. ATG (Air-to-Ground) technology utilizes terrestrial cell towers to provide internet access, ideal for regional flights. Ka Band Satellite offers high-speed connectivity via satellites operating in the Ka frequency range, suitable for long-haul international flights. Ku Band Satellite, on the other hand, uses lower frequency satellites, providing reliable service for a wider range of aircraft but with slightly lower speeds compared to Ka Band.</p></p>
