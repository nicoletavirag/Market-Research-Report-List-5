<p><p>Sugar spheres are small, spherical sugar particles used in pharmaceuticals as inert fillers or binders. They come in two main market types; Microspheres and Standard Spheres. Microspheres are smaller in size and are used for controlled drug release and taste masking. Standard Spheres are larger in size and are primarily used as fillers in tablets or capsules. Both types offer advantages in drug delivery systems, such as improved drug stability, enhanced bioavailability, and controlled release of active ingredients.</p></p>
