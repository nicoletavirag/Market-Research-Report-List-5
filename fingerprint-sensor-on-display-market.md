<p><strong>The Fingerprint Sensor on Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fingerprint Sensor on Display market primarily comprises two types: optical and ultrasonic sensors. Optical sensors capture fingerprints by illuminating the finger and capturing the reflected light. They are typically cost-effective and widely used in smartphones. Ultrasonic sensors, on the other hand, utilize sound waves to create a detailed 3D image of the fingerprint, offering enhanced security and performance, especially under varying conditions. Both technologies aim to provide seamless user authentication while maximizing display aesthetics.</p></p>
