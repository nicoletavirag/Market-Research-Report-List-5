<p><strong>The Muconic Acid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trans, trans-Muconic Acid</li><li>Cis, trans-Muconic Acid</li><li>Cis, cis-Muconic Acid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Muconic acid is primarily categorized into three types based on its geometric isomerism: trans, trans-muconic acid, cis, trans-muconic acid, and cis, cis-muconic acid. Trans, trans-muconic acid is characterized by the arrangement of double bonds that minimize steric hindrance, making it the most stable isomer. Cis, trans-muconic acid exhibits one double bond in a cis configuration, which affects reactivity. Cis, cis-muconic acid has both double bonds in the cis configuration, resulting in different physical and chemical properties, influencing its market applications.</p></p>
