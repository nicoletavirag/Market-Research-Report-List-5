<p><p>The Eutectic Al-Si alloy market is categorized into two primary types: single eutectic and hypereutectic alloys. Single eutectic alloys feature a specific composition that solidifies to form a dual-phase microstructure, providing excellent casting properties and strength for precision applications. In contrast, hypereutectic alloys contain a higher silicon content, which enhances wear resistance and strength, making them ideal for high-performance components like engine parts. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive and aerospace, catering to varying performance requirements.</p></p>
