<p><p>Calibration pumps are essential tools used for testing and calibrating pressure measuring instruments. The market for calibration pumps is primarily divided into two types: hydraulic calibration pumps and pneumatic calibration pumps. Hydraulic calibration pumps utilize liquid, typically oil, to generate pressure, making them suitable for high-pressure applications and providing precise control. In contrast, pneumatic calibration pumps use compressed air to create pressure, ideal for low-pressure scenarios and quick adjustments. Both types cater to specific industry needs, ensuring accurate pressure measurement and compliance.</p></p>
