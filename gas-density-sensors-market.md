<p><p>Gas density sensors are essential for monitoring gas concentrations in various environments. The market comprises two main types: point sensors and open path sensors. Point sensors provide localized measurements, offering precise detection of specific gas concentrations at singular points, ideal for confined spaces. In contrast, open path sensors measure gas concentrations over a longer distance by analyzing the gas present along a defined path, making them suitable for larger areas and allowing for rapid monitoring of gas leaks or emissions.</p></p>
