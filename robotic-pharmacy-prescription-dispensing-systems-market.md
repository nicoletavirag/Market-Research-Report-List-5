<p><p>Robotic pharmacy prescription dispensing systems are categorized into centralized and decentralized dispensing systems. Centralized dispensing systems consolidate medications in a central location, allowing for efficient bulk dispensing and streamlined inventory management. They are typically found in larger hospital settings. In contrast, decentralized dispensing systems place automated dispensers closer to patient care areas, enabling quicker access to medications. This setup enhances workflow and improves patient safety by reducing waiting times and potential medication errors in clinical environments.</p></p>
