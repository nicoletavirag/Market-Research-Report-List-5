<p><p>Tag Management Systems (TMS) are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based TMS are hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, flexibility, and ease of access without the need for extensive IT infrastructure. They often feature automatic updates and integrations with other cloud services. On-premises TMS, on the other hand, are installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure, offering greater control over data and customization but requiring more resources for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
