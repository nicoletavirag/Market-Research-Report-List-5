<p><p>The intravenous access devices market comprises three primary types: intravenous catheters, intravenous infusion pumps, and intravenous needles. Intravenous catheters are flexible tubes inserted into veins for medication or fluid delivery. Intravenous infusion pumps regulate the flow of fluids or drugs, ensuring precise dosing. Intravenous needles provide a temporary means for vascular access, facilitating quick medication administration or blood sampling. Together, these devices are essential for efficient patient care in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare settings.</p></p>
