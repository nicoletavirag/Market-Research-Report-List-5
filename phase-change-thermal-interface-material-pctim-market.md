<p><p>Phase Change Thermal Interface Materials (PCTIM) enhance heat transfer between components by changing their state at specific temperatures. The market consists mainly of thermal pads and thermal paste. Thermal pads are solid materials that become softer when heated, providing consistent heat conduction and ease of application. Thermal paste, on the other hand, is a viscous substance applied between surfaces to fill microscopic gaps, improving thermal contact. Both types are essential for efficient thermal management in electronic devices, ensuring performance and reliability.</p></p>
