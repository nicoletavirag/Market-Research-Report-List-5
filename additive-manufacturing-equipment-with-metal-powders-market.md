<p><p>Additive manufacturing equipment utilizing metal powders primarily includes Selective Laser Melting (SLM), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and other technologies. SLM employs a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powders, creating intricate parts layer by layer with a high degree of precision. EBM utilizes an electron beam in a vacuum environment for melting metal powders, enabling faster build rates and processing of high-temperature materials. Other methods may include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
