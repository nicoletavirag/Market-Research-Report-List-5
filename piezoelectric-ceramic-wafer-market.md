<p><p>The piezoelectric ceramic wafer market includes various types, primarily categorized as Lead Zinc Titanates, Lead Titanate, and Lead Magnesium Niobate. Lead Zinc Titanates are known for their high piezoelectric coefficients and stability, making them suitable for sensors and actuators. Lead Titanate offers excellent electromechanical coupling, ideal for transducers. Lead Magnesium Niobate provides a balance of properties, exhibiting low dielectric losses and good thermal stability, which is beneficial for high-frequency applications. These materials cater to diverse industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
