<p><strong>The Trimellitic Anhydride Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MC Method</li><li>MGC Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trimellitic Anhydride (TMA) is primarily produced through two market methods: the MC Method and the MGC Method. The MC Method focuses on the oxidation of trimethylbenzene, yielding high-purity TMA ideal for coatings and plasticizers. Conversely, the MGC Method involves the extraction of TMA from phthalic anhydride as a byproduct, offering a more economical approach. Both methods cater to different market needs, with the MC Method providing superior quality while the MGC Method emphasizes cost efficiency.</p></p>
