<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) analytics market can be categorized into three data types: structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data. Structured data is highly organized, typically residing in databases, making it easy to analyze, such as sensor readings. Semi-structured data combines elements of both and may include formats like JSON or XML, present in IoT communications. Unstructured data lacks a predefined structure, comprising varied forms like images and videos from devices, posing analytical challenges but offering rich insights into user behavior and operational efficiency.</p></p>
