<p><strong>The 5G Fiber Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Cable</li><li>Multi-Mode Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G fiber optic cables market is divided into two main types: single-mode and multi-mode cables. Single-mode cables, designed for long-distance communication, utilize a single light path, offering higher bandwidth and reduced signal attenuation, making them ideal for extensive networks and high-speed data transmission. In contrast, multi-mode cables support multiple light paths, catering to shorter distances with lower bandwidth requirements. These cables are suitable for local area networks and data centers, optimizing performance and cost-effectiveness in various 5G applications.</p></p>
