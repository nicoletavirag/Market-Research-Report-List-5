<p><p>Glaucoma therapeutics market consists of various types of medications aimed at managing intraocular pressure and preventing optic nerve damage. Prostaglandin analogs work by increasing fluid drainage from the eye. Beta blockers reduce the production of aqueous humor. Alpha agonists decrease fluid production and increase drainage. Carbonic anhydrase inhibitors also reduce aqueous humor production. Each type of medication targets different mechanisms to effectively manage glaucoma and prevent further vision loss. Patients may be prescribed one or a combination of these medications based on their individual condition.</p></p>
