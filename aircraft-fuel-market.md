<p><p>The aircraft fuel market primarily consists of two types: Avgas and jet fuel. Avgas, or aviation gasoline, is used in piston-engine aircraft, predominantly in smaller general aviation planes. It is characterized by its high octane rating. Jet fuel, on the other hand, is designed for turbine-powered aircraft, including commercial jets and military planes. The most common types are Jet A and Jet A-1, known for their kerosene base, ensuring optimal performance at high altitudes and low temperatures.</p></p>
