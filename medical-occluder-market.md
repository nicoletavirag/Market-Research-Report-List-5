<p><strong>The Medical Occluder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PDA Occluder</li><li>ASD Occluder</li><li>VSD Occluder</li><li>LAA Occluder</li><li>PFO Occluder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical occluder market consists of various types catering to specific cardiac conditions. PDA occluders are used to close patent ductus arteriosus; ASD occluders target atrial septal defects; VSD occluders address ventricular septal defects. LAA occluders are designed for left atrial appendage occlusion to prevent stroke in patients with atrial fibrillation, while PFO occluders close patent foramen ovale, reducing the risk of paradoxical embolism. Each type plays a crucial role in congenital heart defect management and stroke prevention.</p></p>
