Dynamic DNA nanotechnology, on the other hand, focuses on utilizing DNA molecules to create dynamic systems that can perform specific functions, such as responsive drug delivery systems or molecular machines. Both types of DNA nanotechnology have a wide range of potential applications in various fields including medicine, electronics, and materials science, driving growth in the DNA nanotechnology market.</p></p>
