<p><p>The automotive privacy window film market consists of three main types: stained film, primary color film, and vacuum coating film. Stained films provide a consistent aesthetic by blocking visibility while maintaining heat rejection properties. Primary color films offer vibrant hues for customization, enhancing the vehicle's appearance. Vacuum coating films, on the other hand, utilize a thin metallic or ceramic layer, providing superior performance in heat reduction and glare control while maintaining clarity. Together, these films enhance both privacy and vehicle aesthetics.</p></p>
