<p><p>Immunoprotein diagnostic testing market includes various types of tests such as C-reactive protein, complement system protein, prealbumin, haptoglobin, immunoglobulin, free light chain, and others. Prealbumin tests assess protein malnutrition, haptoglobin tests detect inflammation and hemolytic anemia, and immunoglobulin tests evaluate immune system health. Free light chain tests help diagnose conditions such as multiple myeloma. Other tests in this market include alpha-1 antitrypsin and ceruloplasmin.</p></p>
