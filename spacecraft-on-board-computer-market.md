<p><p>The spacecraft on-board computer market is categorized into three main segments based on orbital altitude: Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), and Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO). LEO systems are mainly used for satellite communication and imaging applications, benefiting from proximity to Earth. MEO systems typically support navigation and communication satellites, balancing coverage and latency. GEO systems maintain a fixed position relative to the Earth, ideal for communication satellites, enabling continuous service to a specific area.</p></p>
