<p><p>The solventless resins market comprises three primary types based on their formulation. Single component resins are ready-to-use systems that do not require mixing, providing ease and convenience. Two component resins consist of a resin and hardener, requiring mixing before application, allowing for enhanced performance and customization. Three component systems include additional additives for specialized applications, enabling improved characteristics such as adhesion, flexibility, or curing speed, catering to diverse industrial needs. These variations offer flexibility and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
