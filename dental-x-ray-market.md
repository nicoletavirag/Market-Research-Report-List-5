<p><strong>The Dental X-Ray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-rays</li><li>Extraoral X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental X-ray market is primarily divided into two types: intraoral and extraoral X-rays. Intraoral X-rays involve positioning the film or sensor inside the patient's mouth, providing detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding tissues, crucial for diagnostics and treatment planning. In contrast, extraoral X-rays are taken with the film or sensor outside the mouth, capturing broader views of the teeth, jaw, and skull, useful for assessing overall dental health and detecting issues beyond individual teeth.</p></p>
