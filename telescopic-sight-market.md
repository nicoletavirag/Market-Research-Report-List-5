<p><p>Telescopic sight market includes various types of sights such as collimating optical sight and reflex sight. Collimating optical sight, also known as a red dot sight, project a red dot onto the target for quick and precise aiming. Reflex sight, on the other hand, uses a reflective glass to display a reticle on the target without the need for magnification. Both types of sights are popular choices for various shooting activities due to their accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
