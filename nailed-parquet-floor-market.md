<p><p>The nailed parquet floor market comprises mainly two types: engineered and solid wood flooring. Engineered flooring consists of layers of wood veneer over a plywood base, offering enhanced stability and resistance to moisture, making it suitable for various environments. Solid wood flooring, on the other hand, is made from a single piece of hardwood and provides a classic aesthetic and durability but can be affected by humidity. Both types cater to distinct preferences in design, performance, and installation.</p></p>
