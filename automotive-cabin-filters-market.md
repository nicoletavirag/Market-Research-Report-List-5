<p><p>Automotive cabin filters are vital in ensuring clean and fresh air inside a vehicle. There are three main types of cabin filters available in the market - Particle filters, Activated Carbon filters, and Electrostatic filters. Particle filters trap dust, pollen, and other particulate matter. Activated Carbon filters are effective in eliminating odors and harmful gases. Electrostatic filters use static electricity to attract and trap particles. Each type of filter offers unique benefits and caters to specific air quality needs in vehicles.</p></p>
