<p><p>Quantum Cascade Lasers (QCLs) are semiconductor lasers that emit in the infrared spectrum and are widely used in applications such as spectroscopy and sensing. The market for QCLs is primarily segmented into two types: Pulsed Quantum Cascade Lasers and Continuous Wave (CW) Quantum Cascade Lasers. Pulsed QCLs offer high peak power and are ideal for time-resolved measurements, while CW QCLs provide consistent output and are suited for applications requiring stable light sources, such as industrial and medical uses.</p></p>
