<p><p>Clinical tissue expanders are medical devices used primarily in reconstructive surgery to create additional soft tissue. Manufacturers are focusing on developing expanders with adjustable features and incorporating biocompatible materials. As patient preferences evolve, there is an increasing emphasis on personalized and patient-centric approaches in reconstructive surgery, further driving market growth.</p></p>
