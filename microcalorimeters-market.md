<p><p>The microcalorimeters market includes three main types: Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC), Isothermal Microcalorimetry (IMC), and Isothermal Titration Calorimetry (ITC). DSC measures heat flow associated with thermal transitions in materials, making it essential for studying phase changes. IMC quantifies minute heat changes in constant temperature scenarios, often used in biological and chemical research. ITC directly measures heat changes during chemical reactions, providing insights into binding interactions and thermodynamics, which are crucial in drug discovery and formulation development.</p></p>
