<p><p>The non-military riflescope market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Telescopic Sight**: This scope utilizes a series of lenses to magnify distant targets, offering precise aiming for hunting and sport shooting. </p><p>2. **Collimating Optical Sight**: These sights allow the shooter to aim without a traditional reticle, featuring a point of aim projected on a lens, ideal for rapid target acquisition. </p><p>3. **Reflex Sight**: With a single lens and illuminated reticle, reflex sights enable quick aiming and enhanced visibility, commonly used in competitive shooting and tactical scenarios.</p></p>
