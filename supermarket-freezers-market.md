<p><ul><li>Air Cooling</li><li>Direct Cold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Supermarket freezers typically fall into two main types: air cooling and direct cold. Air cooling systems circulate cold air to maintain temperature, allowing for even distribution across shelves and minimizing frost buildup. This method is often more energy-efficient and easier to maintain. In contrast, direct cold systems use refrigerant coils to cool the surfaces directly, providing faster temperature drop and maintaining specific conditions for sensitive products. Each system is designed to optimize product freshness and enhance shopping experience.</p></p>
